Athletic Training Event Coverage

Our event coverage services provide professional athletic training support to ensure the safety of participants at sporting events, tournaments, and competitions.

Professional Event Medical Coverage

Desert Sports Med provides certified athletic trainers for sporting events of all sizes and types. Our athletic trainers are specifically trained to recognize, assess, and manage athletic injuries on the field and provide the safest course of action for your athletes.

Our Event Coverage Includes:

  • On-site Athletic Trainers: Licensed, certified athletic trainers with experience in event coverage.
  • Injury Assessment: Immediate evaluation of injuries that occur during your event.
  • First Aid: Treatment of minor injuries and appropriate referral for more serious conditions.
  • Emergency Management: Coordination with emergency medical services when necessary.
  • Return-to-Play Decisions: Expert guidance on whether injured athletes can safely return to competition.
  • Weather Monitoring: Tracking of environmental conditions and implementation of safety protocols.

Benefits of Professional Event Coverage

Participant Safety

Our Event Coverage Process

1

Initial Consultation

We begin with a detailed discussion about your event, including the type of event, number of participants, venue details, and specific medical coverage needs.

2

Coverage Plan Development

Based on your event details, we develop a comprehensive coverage plan that includes staffing requirements, equipment needs, and emergency action plans.

3

Pre-Event Coordination

We coordinate with your event staff, venue management, and local emergency services to ensure seamless integration of our athletic training services.

4

Event Coverage

Our certified athletic trainers provide professional medical coverage throughout your event, managing injuries, making return-to-play decisions, and ensuring participant safety.

5

Post-Event Reporting

After your event, we provide a detailed report of injuries, treatments provided, and recommendations for future events.
