Medical Tent Services

Professional Medical Tent Coverage

Medical Tents serve as centralized treatment facilities at your event - mini field hospitals strategically positioned to serve all participants with the equipment to handle multiple injuries simultaneously.

Our Services Include:

  • Professional Setup: Treatment tables, privacy screens, and proper lighting
  • Certified Athletic Trainers: Licensed professionals with specialized training
  • Essential Equipment: AEDs, spine boards, and advanced first aid supplies
  • Complete Supplies: Taping, wound care, ice therapy, and OTC medications
  • Emergency Planning: Venue-specific protocols with EMS coordination
  • Documentation: Detailed treatment records and incident reports

Benefits of Medical Tent Services

Centralized Treatment Facility

Fully-equipped treatment centers that handle multiple patients simultaneously in a controlled environment.

Comprehensive Care

Wider range of treatment options than mobile coverage, with more equipment and privacy.

Liability Protection

Reduce your organization's liability with proper medical coverage from licensed professionals.

Event Credibility

Meet requirements for sanctioned events and demonstrate commitment to participant safety.
