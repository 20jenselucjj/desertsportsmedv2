Performance Therapy

Our performance therapy services are designed to help athletes and active individuals optimize their physical capabilities, prevent injuries, and recover faster.

What is Performance Therapy?

Performance Therapy is a specialized approach that combines various manual therapy techniques, movement assessments, and corrective exercises to enhance physical performance and accelerate recovery. Unlike traditional physical therapy that focuses primarily on rehabilitation, Performance Therapy aims to optimize function and prevent injuries before they occur.

Our Approach

At Desert Sports Med, our Performance Therapy services are tailored to each individual's specific needs and goals. We begin with a comprehensive assessment to identify movement patterns, muscle imbalances, and potential areas of concern. Based on this assessment, we develop a personalized treatment plan that may include:

  • Manual Therapy: Hands-on techniques to address soft tissue restrictions, joint mobility, and neuromuscular function.
  • Movement Pattern Correction: Identifying and correcting dysfunctional movement patterns that may lead to injury or decreased performance.
  • Neuromuscular Re-education: Training the nervous system to activate muscles properly and in the correct sequence.
  • Recovery Strategies: Implementing effective recovery protocols to enhance tissue healing and reduce soreness.
  • Performance Enhancement: Specific interventions designed to improve power, endurance, speed, and agility.

Benefits of Performance Therapy

Enhanced Performance

Improve your athletic capabilities and reach new performance levels through optimized movement patterns and increased functional capacity.

Injury Prevention

Identify and address potential issues before they lead to injuries, keeping you active and performing at your best.

Faster Recovery

Accelerate recovery between training sessions and competitions with targeted recovery protocols and interventions.

Improved Body Awareness

Develop a better understanding of your body's mechanics and learn to recognize early warning signs of potential issues.
