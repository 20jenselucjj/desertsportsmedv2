About Desert Sports Med

Meet our team and learn about our mission and approach

Our Story

Desert Sports Med was founded in 2015 with a simple mission: to provide the highest quality sports medicine and rehabilitation services to athletes and active individuals in St. George, Utah.

What began as a small practice has grown into a comprehensive sports medicine center, offering a wide range of services from injury assessment and treatment to performance enhancement and preventive care.

Our approach combines evidence-based treatments with personalized care, ensuring that each client receives the attention and expertise they need to achieve their health and performance goals.

We believe that everyone deserves access to high-quality sports medicine care, whether you're a professional athlete, weekend warrior, or simply someone who wants to stay active and healthy.

Our Mission

At Desert Sports Med, our mission is to empower athletes and active individuals to achieve their full potential through expert sports medicine care, personalized rehabilitation, and performance enhancement programs.

We are committed to:

  • Providing evidence-based treatments that deliver results
  • Offering personalized care that addresses each client's unique needs
  • Creating a supportive environment where clients feel valued and understood
  • Staying at the forefront of sports medicine through ongoing education and research
  • Building lasting relationships with our clients and the community

Our Approach

Personalized Care

We recognize that every client is unique, with different goals, needs, and challenges. That's why we take the time to understand your specific situation and create a personalized treatment plan that addresses your individual needs.

Evidence-Based Treatment

Our treatments are based on the latest research and proven techniques in sports medicine. We stay up-to-date with advances in the field to ensure that our clients receive the most effective care available.

Collaborative Approach

We believe in working together with our clients as partners in their care. We listen to your concerns, involve you in decision-making, and empower you with the knowledge and tools you need to take an active role in your recovery and performance enhancement.

Our Facility

Our state-of-the-art facility in St. George, Utah is designed to provide a comfortable and effective environment for sports medicine care, rehabilitation, and performance training. We've invested in the latest equipment and technology to ensure that our clients receive the highest quality care.
