Team Athletic Training Services

Our team services provide comprehensive athletic training support to keep your athletes healthy, performing at their best, and safely returning to play after injuries.

Comprehensive Team Coverage

Desert Sports Med offers complete athletic training services for teams at all levels, from youth sports to professional organizations. Our certified athletic trainers become an integral part of your team, providing the expertise and support needed to keep your athletes healthy and performing at their best.

Our Team Approach

We work closely with coaches, team physicians, and other staff members to create a collaborative environment focused on athlete health and performance. Our team services include:

  • Sideline Coverage: Professional athletic training presence at practices, games, and team events.
  • Injury Prevention Programs: Team-specific protocols to reduce injury risk and enhance performance.
  • Injury Management: Immediate evaluation and treatment of injuries when they occur.
  • Rehabilitation Services: Comprehensive rehabilitation programs to safely return athletes to play.
  • Communication: Regular updates with coaches, parents, and team physicians regarding athlete status.

Benefits for Teams

Reduced Injury Rates

Proactive prevention strategies and early intervention help minimize time lost to injuries and keep your team at full strength.

Enhanced Performance

Our athletic trainers work with your coaching staff to optimize training programs and improve overall team performance.

Expert Medical Care

Immediate access to qualified healthcare professionals who understand the demands of your sport and can provide appropriate care.

Liability Reduction

Professional medical oversight helps ensure proper protocols are followed, reducing risk and liability for your organization.
