Athletic Training Services

Our athletic training services are designed to help athletes prevent injuries, enhance performance, and recover safely when injuries occur.

What is Athletic Training?

Athletic training encompasses the prevention, examination, diagnosis, treatment, and rehabilitation of emergent, acute or chronic injuries and medical conditions. Athletic trainers are highly qualified, multi-skilled health care professionals who collaborate with physicians to provide preventative services, emergency care, clinical diagnosis, therapeutic intervention and rehabilitation of injuries and medical conditions.

Our Approach

At Desert Sports Med, our athletic training services are provided by certified athletic trainers who are licensed to practice in the state of Utah. We take a comprehensive approach to athletic training that includes:

  • Injury Prevention: Developing strategies to prevent injuries through proper training techniques, equipment fitting, and environmental monitoring.
  • Clinical Evaluation and Diagnosis: Assessing injuries and conditions to determine appropriate treatment and management strategies.
  • Immediate Care: Providing first aid and emergency care for injuries and illnesses.
  • Treatment and Rehabilitation: Designing and implementing rehabilitation programs to facilitate recovery and return to activity.
  • Organization and Administration: Coordinating with coaches, parents, and other healthcare providers to ensure comprehensive care.
